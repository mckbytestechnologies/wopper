Who We Are
A legacy of quality, a future of innovation

Wopper Cleaning Solutions is an innovative new segment launched by Shri Meera Labs Pvt Ltd, a reputable company known for its diverse range of products. Wopper specializes in providing a wide variety of cleaning solutions tailored to both residential and commercial needs.

With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, Wopper Cleaning Solutions is committed to establishing itself as a trusted name in the cleaning industry by offering reliable products that cater to the evolving demands of consumers. This new segment started in 2017, signifies Shri Meera Labs Pvt Ltd’s expansion into the cleaning sector, reflecting their dedication to growth and excellence across multiple product categories.

Founded in 2017
The Parent Company
Shri Meera Labs Pvt Ltd — Leaders in Specialty Chemicals

Shri Meera Labs Private Limited is one of the leading manufacturers of Specialty Chemicals based in Chennai, catering to the needs of a variety of industries across India and globally.

We are an ISO 9001 Company, certified by TUV India, a member of TUV Nord, Germany. Our products are of international quality, competitive, eco-friendly, biodegradable and are totally compliant with norms for a pollution-free environment.
