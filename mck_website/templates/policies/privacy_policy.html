Introduction
Our commitment to your privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how Shri Meera Labs Private Limited ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our website, wopper.store (the "Site").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

By using our Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
Information We Collect
What data we gather from you

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to improve our services to you.

Personal Information

We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and payment information, when you:

  • Create an account on the Site.
  • Place an order.
  • Subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Contact us for customer support.
  • Participate in surveys or promotions.

Usage Data

We may also collect usage data, such as your browsing history, search queries, and IP address, when you use the Site. This information helps us understand how you use the Site and improve your experience.

Personal Information Name, email, address, phone, payment details
Usage Data Browsing history, search queries, IP address
Cookies Session data, preferences, analytics
Device Information Device type, browser, operating system
We collect this information to provide you with a better shopping experience and to improve our services.
How We Use Your Information
The purposes for which we process your data

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To process your orders and provide you with the products and services you request.
  • To send you important information about your orders, including order confirmations and shipping notifications.
  • To send you marketing communications, such as newsletters and promotions, with your consent. (You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications at any time.)
  • To personalize your experience on the Site.
  • To improve the Site and our services.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security.
We will not use your personal information for purposes other than those stated in this policy without your explicit consent.
Sharing Your Information
When and with whom we share your data

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Site and provide our services, such as:

  • Payment Processors - To securely process your payments.
  • Shipping Companies - To deliver your orders.
  • Marketing Agencies - To help us with promotional activities.
  • IT Service Providers - To maintain and improve our systems.

These third-party service providers are obligated to use your information only to perform services on our behalf and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ourselves or others.

We never sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.
Data Retention
How long we keep your information

We will retain your information for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

We retain your personal information for:

  • As long as you have an active account with us.
  • As long as needed to provide you with our services.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • To resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.
When we no longer need your personal information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.
Your Choices
You have control over your information

You have several choices regarding your information:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your personal information in your account settings.
  • Opt-out of Marketing: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or by contacting us.
  • Request Deletion: You can request that we delete your information by contacting us.
  • Cookie Preferences: You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@wopper.in
Children's Privacy
Our policy regarding children under 13

Our Site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. We will take steps to remove the information from our records.

If you are under the age of 13, please do not use our Site or provide any personal information to us.
Security
How we protect your information

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption - For secure data transmission.
  • Secure Servers - Protected by firewalls and access controls.
  • Regular Security Audits - To identify and address vulnerabilities.
  • Access Controls - Limited to authorized personnel only.
No internet transmission or electronic storage is ever completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information, but we strive to protect it to the best of our ability.
Cookies
How we use cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Site and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic functioning of the Site.
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors interact with the Site.
  • Marketing Cookies: To deliver relevant advertisements.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Site.

By continuing to use our Site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
