Ordering & Support

You can place an order by following these simple steps:

  1. Browse the website and add desired products to your cart.
  2. Review your cart and proceed to the checkout page.
  3. Enter your shipping address and select a payment method.

You will receive an order confirmation email once your order is successfully placed.

All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway partners.

Website Navigation

To create an account:

  1. Click on the Login/Register button at the top of the page.
  2. Fill in your name, email address, and password.
  3. Click Register to complete the process.

You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

You can track your order by:

  1. Logging into your account and visiting the My Orders section.
  2. Clicking on the order number to view the tracking details.
  3. Using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email.

You will receive real-time updates on your delivery status.

To reset your password:

  1. Click on Login and select Forgot Password?
  2. Enter your registered email address.
  3. You will receive a password reset link via email.
  4. Click the link and follow the instructions to set a new password.
